Smalltalk

Smalltalk is a social game where conversation is the whole point. You arrive somewhere temporary, meet a cast of characters, and build relationships through what you actually say to them. No scripted dialogue trees. Every conversation is real, freeform, and shapes what happens next.

The people you meet have their own personalities, moods, and boundaries. Pay attention and be genuine, and something real develops. Be careless, and they'll drift away. Here's the twist: not everyone you're talking to is who you think they are. Some characters might be played by other people. Can you tell the difference? Does it matter?

There's no score and no winning. Just the story that unfolds from how you choose to spend your time and who you choose to spend it with. We're keeping the finer details close to the chest for now, but if you like games that reward curiosity and conversation over reflexes and min-maxing, this one's for you. About Koala Works

Koala Works is a bootstrapped tech startup based in Australia. We split our time between two things we care about: building games that bring people together, and helping other companies build great software.

On the consulting side, we offer software engineering services with deep expertise in modern agentic AI systems (think tools like Claude Code and frameworks like Openclaw), alongside general full-stack development. We're especially good at helping teams figure out where AI agents can actually add value, not just where they sound cool on a slide deck.

On the games side, we make role-playing and social games that lean on what makes real human interaction interesting. Smalltalk is our first release.
